Why cloud ?
Today companies are having great concern over day to day costing involved in their datacenter . They are looking at a more resourceful and economical way that can help perform their business operations in an effective way at low infrastructure and systems cost. The following key drivers have pushed enterprises to shift to cloud:

Pay only for what you use

  • Reduction in infrastructure and infrastructure management needs
  • Quick implementation of IT services and / or business processes
  • Provides access to the latest version of applications
  • Lack of in-house skills to implement and manage

However the challenges for businesses in adopting the right strategy for cloud computing are immense as they have to traverse a minefield of different models (public vs. private vs. hybrid), different types of service providers (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS etc.) and also assess the suitability in the context of their own business and IT organizations. In addition, constraints like limited exposure and expertise restrict the ability to adopt cloud services for their business. This emphasizes the need to work with the right solution partner who can bring a strong understanding of the business, expertise and knowledge required for developing architecture and designs the best migration strategy that suits the business - IT alignment necessary to develop the best fit cloud solution. Brume with rich heritage in consultative approach to solving problems and vast experience in transformational programs is ideally positioned to businesses navigate this exciting journey of cloud enabling their IT assets. Also, with proven capabilities in cloud service offerings, Brume is ideally positioned to offer cloud based solutions.
